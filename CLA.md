## TL;DR

**You keep your copyright.** This CLA just gives us permission to include your contributions in both our open-source (AGPL) and commercial versions of AgentFlow. Sign once, contribute forever.

## Why We Need This

AgentFlow uses an "Open Core" business model: the core is open-source (AGPL-3.0), but we also offer a commercial/enterprise version with additional features. This CLA allows us to include community contributions in both versions, keeping the project sustainable while staying open-source.
